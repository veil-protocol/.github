# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

VEIL Protocol is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive environment for everyone interested in privacy technology.

## Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** — Treat others as you wish to be treated
- **Be constructive** — Focus on technical merit and facts
- **Be collaborative** — Work together toward better solutions
- **Be patient** — Remember that people have varying levels of expertise
- **Assume good faith** — Interpret others' statements charitably

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Sharing others' private information
- Spam or off-topic promotion
- Disrupting productive discussion

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:
- GitHub repositories (issues, PRs, discussions)
- Official communication channels
- Project-related events

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
1. Warning
2. Temporary ban
3. Permanent ban

Reports will be handled confidentially.

## Focus

This project exists to advance privacy technology. Political discussions, while sometimes relevant, should focus on technical implications rather than partisan positions.

We believe privacy is a fundamental right that transcends political boundaries.
